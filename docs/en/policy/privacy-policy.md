# Privacy Policy

This page explains what data is collected, the reasons for its collection, and how that data is managed.

## Data Collection

Data collected from accounts and platform usage is limited to what is necessary to operate and improve the service. This may include account identity, basic usage metrics, and technical information such as IP addresses.

## Data Usage

Collected data is used to operate the platform, maintain security, and understand how features are used. Data is not sold, nor is it shared with third parties beyond what is necessary to operate the service itself.

## Data Protection

Data is stored using industry-standard access controls, and access is restricted to the systems and personnel directly involved in operating the platform.

## Your Rights

Account holders may request a copy of their stored data or request data deletion, with the exception of information that must be retained for legal or security reasons.

## Policy Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be reflected in the "last updated" date at the bottom of this page.
